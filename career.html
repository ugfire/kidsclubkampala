For several years, KCK offers the best and excellent Internship and Graduate Trainee (IGTs) Program for regional and international students.

Kids Club Kampala has been in the business of international volunteers and internship management, an experience it has gained practically through learning. Our partnerships among others are; the University of Sheffield, Manchester University, London South Bank University, Makerere University, Kyambogo University, Ndejje University and churches; Catalyst vineyard church (Scotland), St Lukeâ€™s church London, and many more across the globe, we have worked with teams from the UK, USA, Norway, Denmark, Sweden, Australia, Korea, Japan, among others. Overtime we have identified the need to re-think and improve in our field experience for both volunteers, internees and graduate trainees.

Benefits

  1. KCK provides the Internees and Graduate Trainees with a conducive research, study and working environment.
  2. An organized local lecture facilitated by lecturers from local universities e.g. Makerere University and Ndejje universities, a chance to interact with a local lecturer exposes the students to more local literature and information from an academic perspective.
  3. Internees are assigned a supervisor who reviews and checks on the students regularly for progressive learning and include such performance observations into the final report.
  4. Overall exposure to all Kids Club Kampala projects and dynamics of operation, plus a chance to interact with some of our stakeholders.
  5. Well established and affordable internship program, inclusive of accommodation and organized weekend tours around the city and the country at large.
