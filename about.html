About us

About us

Kids Club Kampala (KCK) is a Christian based charity Organization founded in 2009 by three friends; Sam, Olivia, Corrie. We are registered in Uganda under NGO number MIA/NB/2010/04/2497, the United Kingdom and the Republic of Ireland.

Our Vision

A world in which hope and love to the vulnerable children transforms communities.

Join Us

Our Mission

Availing children safe places to play while catering for their physical, emotional and spiritual needs as well as empowering them to realise their God given potential.

Where we work

We currently work in a total of 10 Kampala slums and other parts of Uganda; our early childhood education project which is a free non formal project is currently running in the three slums of Katanga, Naguru and Namuwongo. Our two skilling centers are carpentry and joinery located in Katanga and sewing and knitting located in Naguru bank zone. Our transitional home for abandoned children is located in Busiika, Luwero district but serves communities in the entire region. Our safeguarding projects inclusive of kids clubs, take place in 10 communities, in 5 districts of Kampala, Wakiso, Mpigi, Pallisa and Luweero.
